## 摘要
间歇性需求波动给灾害物流和医疗供应系统带来了重大挑战。在本研究中，我们将累积需求建模为由漂移项、泊松跳跃和复合泊松跳跃组成的广义 Lévy 过程，并分析了一个连续时间库存模型。所提出的框架提供了一个统一的公式，涵盖了带漂移的泊松过程和带漂移的复合泊松过程。从数学角度来看，我们将再订货时间视为从属过程的首达时问题，并通过拉普拉斯指数推导出其拉普拉斯变换。特别地，对于带漂移的泊松情形，我们利用 Lambert W 函数获得了拉普拉斯指数逆函数的显式表示，从而得到了首达时拉普拉斯变换的解析表达式。此外，当跳跃大小服从指数分布和 Gamma 分布时，我们推导出了再订货时间均值和方差的显式公式，从而阐明了广义 Lévy 需求过程首达时的矩结构。从运筹学角度来看，我们根据累积需求的分布，明确刻画了有限时间范围内的预期总成本。本研究提出了一个将 Lévy 过程的首达时理论与连续时间库存控制相结合的分析框架。

## Abstract
Intermittent demand fluctuations pose significant challenges in disaster logistics and medical supply systems. In this study, we formulate cumulative demand as a generalized Lévy process composed of a drift term, Poisson jumps, and compound Poisson jumps, and analyze a continuous-time inventory model. The proposed framework provides a unified formulation that encompasses both drifted Poisson processes and drifted compound Poisson processes.   From a mathematical perspective, we treat the reorder time as a first-passage problem of a subordinator and derive its Laplace transform via the Laplace exponent. In particular, for the drifted Poisson case, we obtain an explicit representation of the inverse Laplace exponent using the Lambert W function, which yields an analytic expression for the Laplace transform of the first-passage time. Furthermore, when the jump sizes follow exponential and Gamma distributions, we derive explicit formulas for the mean and variance of the reorder times, thereby clarifying the moment structure of first-passage times for generalized Lévy demand processes.   From an operations research perspective, we explicitly characterize the expected total cost over a finite time horizon based on the distribution of cumulative demand. This study presents an analytical framework that integrates first-passage theory of Lévy processes with continuous-time inventory control.
